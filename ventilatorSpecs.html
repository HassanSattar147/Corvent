Wide range of functionality

Corvent offers 8 modes of operation namely PC-CMV, VC-CMV, PC-A/C, VC-A/C, PC-SIMV, AC-SIMV, PSV & PRVC. It also provides time, pressure, and flow triggers while the breaths are time or flow cycled.

Accurate Oxygen saturation control

Corvent ensures accurate oxygen saturation delivery through an ingeniously designed proportional valve. It does not require any flow sensors or electronic variable valves

Accurate Tidal Volume delivery

Corvent utilizes piston-cylinder mechanism to ensure accurate tidal volume delivery. An 800ml cylinder ensures that all the displaced volume gets into lung which is otherwise not possible by controlling volume through variable valves in pneumatic system

Safety features

Corvent performs self-check tests for any leakage, failure of electrical or mechanical component or for gas supply connection as the first step after it is switched on. It also offers wide range of alarms if any parameter is exceeded beyond limit and alarms as well as provides alternative solution in case of power failure or gas supply failure

Air filter regulator

Corvent utilizes air filter regulators whose filters can be changed upto any size to ensure delivery of pure medical air. It also utilizes N95 filters in inhale and exhale circuits to prevent any contamination
